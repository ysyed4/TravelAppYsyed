Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

Introduction

Our app is a platform for exploring European destinations, creating destination lists, and sharing reviews. To maintain a safe and welcoming environment for all users, we require that all users comply with this Acceptable Use Policy.

Prohibited Activities

Users must not:

  • Post content that is illegal, harmful, defamatory, or discriminatory.
  • Infringe upon the rights of others, including intellectual property rights.
  • Use the app to harass, bully, or intimidate others.
  • Attempt to bypass or undermine security features.
  • Use the app for any unlawful or unauthorized purpose.

User-Generated Content

You are responsible for the content you post, including destination lists and reviews. By posting content, you agree that it complies with this policy and does not infringe upon the rights of any third party.

Enforcement

We reserve the right to remove content, disable accounts, or take other action if we believe a user has violated this policy. We may also report illegal activities to the appropriate authorities.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or wish to report a violation, please contact us at aup@yourdomain.com.
